# Code of Conduct

Chutes Build contributors and maintainers are expected to keep project spaces
professional, respectful, and technically constructive.

Acceptable participation includes giving actionable feedback, disagreeing on
the merits, respecting privacy and licensing boundaries, and helping newcomers
understand project conventions. Harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing,
deliberate disruption, sexualized conduct, or publishing another person's
private information is not acceptable.

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and restrict participation when
necessary to protect the community or project. Reports should use an available
private maintainer contact or, for sensitive security matters, the process in
[SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md). Reports will be handled as confidentially and
consistently as practical.

This policy applies in repository discussions, reviews, project communication,
and public representation of the project.
